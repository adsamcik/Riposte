Riposte helps you organize, search, and share memes from your personal collection — fast.

Tag your memes with emojis, search by mood or description, and share directly to any app in seconds. Built for the moment you're mid-conversation and need the perfect reaction image.

Features:

• Emoji-based categorization — tap an emoji to find matching memes instantly
• AI-powered semantic search — describe what you're looking for in plain words
• Quick share — long-press any meme to share it directly
• Import from gallery or ZIP bundles — bring your whole collection in at once
• Customizable sharing — control format, quality, and size before sending
• Fully offline — all AI processing happens on-device, no data leaves your phone
• Material You — dynamic colors that match your system theme

Riposte is free and open source software.
